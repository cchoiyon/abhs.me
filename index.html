About Us

Anirban Bangladesh Hindu Society is a religious organization of Bangladeshi Hindu community living in Hatfield, Pennsylvania, USA. The organization was founded in 2004 to give Bangladeshi Hindu community of Pennsylvania a social space to perform religious and cultural activities in a safe, secure and inclusive atmosphere. Anirban Bangladesh Hindu Society is a not-for-profit, non-political and open to all. The organization’s prime focus is to pass the teachings and tradition of Hindu religion to the next generation of Bangladeshis. Each year, the organization arranges Puja festivals, picnics, music, dance events and other activities for its members.
